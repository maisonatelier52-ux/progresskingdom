Privacy Policy

Progress Kingdom Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 2025

Progress Kingdom collects, uses, distributes, and protects personal information on our U.S.–based digital news platform, including our website, mobile-accessible pages, and any service that links to this Privacy Policy. You agree to this Privacy Policy by using our Services.

1. Privacy Policy Scope

This Privacy Policy covers any information gathered whether you read articles, submit forms, contact us, comment, interact with adverts, or visit our website. Not covered by this policy:

  • Information acquired offline or by phone unless stated.
  • Websites, social platforms, and apps that link to or integrate with our Services process data.
  • Content or data covered by different agreements.

2. The information we collect

A. Your Direct Information

If you want to, we might ask for the following personal information:

  • Contact Information: Name, email, and phone number
  • Account or subscription data: User name and password for the account (if you have them)
  • Comments & submissions: Messages, comments, and form submissions
  • Newsletters: Email preferences for newsletters and subscriptions

B. Data That Is Collected Automatically

When you use our Services, we automatically collect:

  • IP and general location
  • Details about the browser, device, and OS
  • Page views, scrolling, and clicking
  • Links for referrals
  • Length of session
  • Data from cookies and analytics

C. Data from Other Sources

  • Analytics partners Analytics services (like Google Analytics)
  • Advertising partners Networks for advertising
  • Social media platforms Interacting with our pages on social media

3. What We Do with Your Information

We use the information we collect:

  • Keep our site up and running
  • Publishing and distributing news
  • Make the site work better and be easier to use
  • Look at the traffic and engagement
  • Respond to messages and questions
  • Stay away from fraud and illegal activity
  • Make ads and content your own
  • Enforcement of the Terms of Use and the law

4. Sharing Information

No one sells personal information. We might share information with:

  • Service providers: Website hosts and companies that keep them safe
  • Advertising partners: Analytics and performance
  • Legal entities: Partners for distributing and measuring ads
  • Business transfers: When necessary, legal authorities Partners in a merger or acquisition in Progresskingdom

5. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and trackers to:

  • Improve how the site works
  • How to measure engagement
  • Ads that are tailored to you
  • What you like to buy

You can turn off cookies, but some features may not work.

6. Your rights to privacy

Depending on where you are, you can ask for:

  • Get to personal data
  • Remove your data
  • Correct incorrect data
  • Don't click on ads that are tailored to you.
  • Limit the data used for targeted ads

Email privacy@progresskingdom.com

7. Protect data

Encryption, monitoring, firewalls, and access limits keep your data safe. There is no safe system; sending data is dangerous.

8. Links and services from outside

Our platform might link to or include tools from other companies. We are not responsible, so we suggest that you look at their privacy policies.

9. Privacy for Kids

Not knowingly getting personal information from kids under 13. If found, the data will be deleted.

10. Users from other countries

We run Progresskingdom from the United States. If you use our Services from outside the U.S., your data may be subject to U.S. privacy laws.

11. Keep Data

We only keep personal data for as long as we need it:

  • Provide services
  • Following the law
  • Settle arguments
  • Be safe
  • Different types of data stay around for different amounts of time.

12. Changes to the Privacy Policy

Policy may change from time to time. The "Last Updated" date shows when the last change was made. By using our services, you agree to the new policy.

13. Get in Touch

Send an email to privacy@progresskingdom.com
